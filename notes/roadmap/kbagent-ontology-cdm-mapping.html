SaaS Extension Mapping (Non-breaking)
- Goal maps to Policy + Project aggregate view.
- KBDoc maps to Module + Evidence documentation edges.
- Signal maps to Evidence with event severity metadata.
- Lesson maps to Evidence linked to Intent retrospectives.
Rule: extensions may exist in downstream views, but export/import contracts remain anchored on the 10 canonical entities.
Bi-temporal Documentation Model
Required frontmatter fields for KBDoc records:
time_state: as_is | to_be
last_updated: YYYY-MM-DD
last_verified: YYYY-MM-DD
verification: unverified | design-only | code-verified
This closes the temporal gap by distinguishing observed current state from target-state intent and verification confidence.
